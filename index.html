📊 Policy Index Calculator

Policy Index Formula

Pi = (ccn × hr) / (a × k) × (1/t)
Where:
• ccn = Cumulative checkbox number (total policies implemented)
• hr = Human resource index (%)
• a = Library age (years)
• k = Consistency block time (5 years)
• t = Time taken to perform policy procedures
